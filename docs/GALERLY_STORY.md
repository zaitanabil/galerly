# Galerly: The Complete Story

## Executive Summary

Galerly is a professional gallery management platform built specifically for photographers who need to share, manage, and deliver photos to their clients. Born from the real needs of working photographers, Galerly transforms the chaotic process of photo delivery into a streamlined, professional experience.

## The Problem We Solve

### Before Galerly

Photographers face daily challenges:
- **Email Chaos**: Sending photos via email means file size limits, broken attachments, and endless back-and-forth
- **Client Confusion**: Clients struggle to view, select, and provide feedback on photos
- **Unprofessional Experience**: Generic file-sharing tools (Dropbox, Google Drive) lack photography-specific features
- **Time Wasted**: Hours spent managing multiple galleries, tracking client selections, and organizing feedback
- **Lost Revenue**: No built-in tools to collect payments or showcase portfolio work
- **Brand Dilution**: Generic platforms don't reflect the photographer's professional brand

### After Galerly

Photographers gain:
- **Professional Galleries**: Beautiful, fast-loading galleries that showcase their work
- **Client Collaboration**: Clients can favorite photos, leave comments, and make selections easily
- **Brand Control**: Custom email templates and white-label experience (Pro plan)
- **Time Savings**: Automated notifications, batch operations, and organized client management
- **Revenue Tools**: Integrated payment collection and portfolio showcasing
- **Peace of Mind**: Secure storage, automatic backups, and reliable delivery

## Appendix: Technical Specifications

### Infrastructure
- **Cloud Provider**: AWS
- **Backend**: Python 3.9, serverless Lambda functions
- **Database**: DynamoDB (NoSQL)
- **Storage**: S3 with CloudFront CDN
- **Email**: SES (Simple Email Service)
- **Payments**: Stripe (ready for integration)

### Security & Compliance
- **Encryption**: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **Authentication**: JWT tokens, bcrypt password hashing
- **Compliance**: GDPR-ready, SOC 2 preparation underway
- **Backups**: Automatic daily snapshots, 30-day retention

### Performance
- **API Response Time**: <200ms average
- **Upload Speed**: Multi-part uploads, parallel processing
- **Image Processing**: Automatic thumbnail/medium generation
- **Scalability**: Auto-scaling to handle traffic spikes

### Browser Support
- Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
- Mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Chrome Android)
