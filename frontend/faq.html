Photography Gallery FAQ - Everything You Need to Know

Common questions about Galerly photography gallery platform. Launched December 2025. Find answers about account creation, gallery management, photo uploads, pricing plans, billing, security, and platform features.

Don't see your question? We're building this FAQ as we go. Contact us anytime.

GETTING STARTED — BASICS

How do I create my first gallery?

After signing in, click "New Gallery" in the navigation. Upload your photos, add client details, and customize settings. Your gallery is ready to share in minutes.

What file formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, and HEIC formats. For best quality, we recommend uploading full-resolution images. Max file size is 50MB per image.

Can I customize my gallery design?

Yes! Pro and Business plans include custom branding, logo upload, color schemes, and custom domains. Starter plan uses our default beautiful design.

BILLING & PLANS — PRICING

Can I change plans?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes are instant. If downgrading, unused time is credited to your account.

Is there a free trial?

Starter is free forever. Professional and Business plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No questions asked. Your galleries and data stay available for 30 days after cancellation for easy export.

Annual discount?

Save 20% with annual billing. That's over 2 months free. Switch from monthly to annual anytime.

What payment methods?

All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfer for annual plans. All payments are secure and encrypted.

How is storage calculated?

Storage includes your original photos plus system-generated files. When you upload images, we create multiple optimized versions for fast loading across devices, thumbnail previews, and watermarked copies for client approval workflows. This processing ensures your galleries load instantly while maintaining quality. The total storage used reflects all versions combined.

TECHNICAL — FEATURES & SECURITY

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure cloud storage (AWS/Google Cloud), daily backups, and are fully GDPR compliant.

Can clients download photos?

Yes. You control download settings per gallery. Choose to allow full resolution, or disable downloads entirely.

Need custom storage?

Contact us for custom plans. We can build enterprise solutions with unlimited storage, dedicated servers, and priority support.

Do you have a mobile app?

Not yet, but our web platform is fully responsive and works beautifully on all devices. Mobile apps are coming in 2025.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely. Download all your photos, client data, and gallery settings anytime. We believe your data is yours.

What about gallery expiration?

You control expiration dates. Set galleries to expire after a date, or keep them live forever. Clients receive reminder emails before expiration.
